What We Do

Our work is to help the African society in the following aspects

Education

We raise awareness to university students about self employment when are in university and after graduating. Because more than 75% of the university students do not get formal jobs after finishing the university. We are going to raise their talents and buisness ideas in order to combact the unemployment rate. To support our work, visit our DONATE page

Charity

"we don't need to have deep pockets or be rich to help the needy, the poor and the hungry, we need to have heart" said Kevin Dcruz. Through our NGO we will be helping the special groups such as street children, aged people who are in special camps, disabled, people who can not afford paying their health treatments, students who cannot afford their school fees etc. Our charity tours will be conducted 8 times in a year. For charity donations, visit our DONATE page

Youth Motivation & Jobs/ Interns

On our blog page, we will be posting different stories of African youth who have attained success while at young age, so as to motivate and inspire other African youth to strive for success. Visit our BLOG for more informations. Also on our blog page we will be posting different jobs and tenders so as to help the youth gaining experience. Visit our Jobs & Interns for more informations.

Our Impact

This is output of our work. For more informations, visit our BLOG page

Through our charity work, we rise the standard of living of different people in Africa and bring happiness to their life

Through rising awareness on self-employee to university students,we help them create their own jobs.

Sharing experience such as vacations, life experience etc, increases feeling of self-esteem and decreases feeling of deepression, anxiety and isolation.
